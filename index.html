Milestones in The History of Technology

The Avancements of the Camera

Cameras haven't always been digital and instant, they have had many different looks throughout the years. The first camera was successful in the mid-1820's, where it was a peice of paper covered in silver chloride in a wooden box that captured an image when exposed to light then closed into darkness. It took them many tries in order to successfully capture one image. Throughout the years after that improvements were made and images were easier to obtain. Fast foward to 1948 and the first instant image camera was created, this was called, "Polaroid". Polaroid would go on to become a very famous and popular camera providing the user the picture directly after taking it. Later in the 1900's camera were able to have film where you could go to stores such as walgreens and have them developed. Now in modern times, cameras are found everywhere and save the image digitally and easily. The camera came from humble beginnings, but some of the original technology still survives today.

How the IPhone Has Changed Over the Years

Since being announced in 2007, the IPhone has had many changes made to it. It has one of the most popular phones known to use, and is a complex computer that is able to be held in your hand. The original iphone provided the user with a touchscreen phone that could be used to make calls, texts, and open and send eamils. The next iphone, known as the 3G, included support for 3G. Every year since the original iphone, apple comes out with a newer one always improving and moving foward. Their next project, the iphone 4, provided a selfie camera which set the standard of gadgets and abilities to have for all phones higher. The iphone 4 was even more of a hit then the 3G, and provided more for the user. Every iphone every year tries to come out with a bigger screen, better camera, and provides a better expierence for the user. Along with the iphone getting improvements every year, apple also adds accessories to the phone. The most recent accessories was the apple airpods, which were a profound hit and bought by most apple users. Throughout its continuous time, apple has been on top of the technology world for phones, and has now entered its second generation for its 10'th phone. The iphone is known as an icon in technology history.
